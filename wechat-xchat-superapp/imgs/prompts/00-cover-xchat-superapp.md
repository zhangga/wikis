Create a polished editorial cover illustration for a Chinese WeChat article about XChat and the idea of a western super app.

Subject:
- A dramatic smartphone screen showing a sleek black XChat interface in the center.
- Around it, subtle visual echoes of messaging, payments, AI assistant, creator economy, and social feed orbit toward one hub.
- The mood should suggest "the closest Elon Musk has come to a western WeChat moment."

Composition:
- Cinematic, high-contrast, clean layout.
- Central phone in portrait orientation with glowing conversation bubbles.
- Background is a layered dark graphite to electric blue gradient with data-grid texture.
- Add floating minimal icons for chat, wallet, AI, and network graph, but keep them elegant and not cluttered.

Style:
- Premium magazine cover illustration.
- Sharp, modern, serious, high-finish.
- Not a generic sci-fi poster. It should feel like a major tech story cover.

Text handling:
- Do not add large readable text in the image.
- Leave a comfortable focal area for future title overlay if needed.

Output:
- 16:9 aspect ratio
- High detail
- Clean edges
- Rich lighting
