---
title: Three scoreboards
purpose: 给读者一个能带走的判断框架
---

Create a shareable infographic for a Chinese WeChat article that teaches readers how to judge whether a super app can really become dominant.

Core framework:
- Three scoreboards:
  1. Relationship migration
  2. Closed-loop connection
  3. Trust accumulation

Visual structure:
- Three large dashboard panels or scoreboards in one frame.
- Each panel has its own icon system and meter.
- Relationship migration: people and message nodes moving from old apps into one new hub.
- Closed-loop connection: chat, wallet, creator, service modules linking into one loop.
- Trust accumulation: shield, lock, reputation, long-term retention curve.

Design feel:
- Practical and screenshot-friendly.
- The reader should instantly feel this is a framework worth saving.
- Minimal short labels, clear hierarchy, confident layout.

Style:
- Premium infographic
- Deep navy / graphite background
- Blue, teal, and restrained amber highlights
- Clean, modern, precise

Output:
- 16:9
- High clarity
- Strong visual hierarchy
